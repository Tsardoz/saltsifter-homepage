Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SaltSifter

How accurate is the AI plant analysis?

Our AI combines computer vision with feeding history analysis for comprehensive plant health assessment. Unlike basic plant ID apps, we focus specifically on nutrition-related issues.

Do I need chemistry knowledge to use SaltSifter?

Not at all. SaltSifter handles all the complex calculations. You just follow the simple recipe instructions - no chemistry knowledge required.

What plants does SaltSifter support?

We support all major vegetables, herbs, flowers, and specialty crops including cannabis. Our database includes optimal nutrition profiles for 50+ plant varieties.

How is this different from Hydrobuddy?

Hydrobuddy requires chemistry expertise and only calculates what you tell it. SaltSifter optimizes recipes automatically and adds plant health analysis with remediation guidance.

Why not just use liquid nutrients?

Liquid nutrients cost 10x more than raw salts and use generic formulas. SaltSifter creates custom recipes optimized for your specific plants and conditions.

How is this different from 'Picture This' plant apps?

SaltSifter focuses on two key areas: recipe cost optimization to save money on nutrients, and superior plant diagnosis by combining plant photos with your feeding history. Picture This just identifies plants and basic problems, while SaltSifter provides actionable solutions specifically for controlled environment agriculture, not general plant identification.

How does the learning system work while protecting my privacy?

SaltSifter learns in two ways: Personal learning uses your private data to optimize for YOUR specific setup. Global learning uses anonymous, aggregated outcome data (like "this nutrition profile increased yields by 20%") to improve recommendations for everyone. We never share your proprietary recipes or personal growing data - only anonymized performance metrics that benefit the entire community.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share your plant data. Your growing information stays private and secure.
